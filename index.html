As a non-custodial wallet, we do not store your private keys or digital assets. We may collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, or contact details when you reach out or use third-party processors.
  • KYC Data: Minimal data from payment processors like MoonPay and Transak for fiat-to-crypto transactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and enhance the Services

We use industry-standard encryption, but you are responsible for securing your private keys.

5. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at support@freedomxwallet.com.
